Pewlett-Hackard

Joining multiple data files into correlating tables from the fictional company Pewlett Hackard to determine the number of retiring employees per title and identify employees who may be eligible to participate in a mentorship program.

Github Repo

Project Overview

The purpose of this project was to determine the number of retiring employees, according to job title, at Pewlett-Hackard. These employees were then further assessed to determine which employees may be the most ideal to participate in an employee mentorship program.

In order to accomplish this analysis, several csv files were joined to collect the appropriate information into datasets that would be more easily read by the CEO of the company. This task also allowed me to become more familiar with databases and relationships between multiple tables. The information from this analysis will help the company prepare for the future as it was discovered that a high percentage of employees could retire at any moment. Additionally, the analysis also displayed which job titles would be hit hardest with retirements.
