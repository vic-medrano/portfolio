MechaCar

Analysis to review production data for a new fictional car prototype using multiple linear regressions, t-tests, and summary statistics,

Code Link

Project Overview

Fictional car company AutosRUs created MechaCar which is currently having production troubles. An analytical review was conducted to provide insight into the company. The main goal of this review was to discover which variables predict the MPG for vehicle prototypes through logistic regression. Additionally, the review wanted to determine if any of the manufacturing lots are statistically different from the mean population.

The linear regression was significant and it was discovered that two variables in the dataset had a non-random effect on the MPG of the MechaCar. However, the regression also indicated that there may be other factors that are not included in the dataset that may have a strong impact. An analysis of the summary statistics indicated that one of the manufacturing lots did not meet specifications and was well over the acceptable variance for PSI. Further inspection of the manufacturing lots indicated that Lots 2 and 3 were statistically different from the population mean in their suspension coils.
